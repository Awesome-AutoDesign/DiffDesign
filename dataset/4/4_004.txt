The design employs an open layout with expansive glass windows, allowing ample natural light to flood the space and create a bright, airy, and welcoming atmosphere. The minimalist cement walls and flooring blend seamlessly with the warm, earthy tones of the wooden furniture, establishing a modern yet inviting environment. The careful use of natural materials, including wood and stone, further enhances the overall sense of comfort, simplicity, and timeless elegance, fostering a harmonious connection between the interior and its surroundings.
