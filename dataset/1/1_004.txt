The interior color scheme draws inspiration from the luminous gas flames associated with oil exploration and production, utilizing color gradients to establish a distinct identity throughout the floor. A dedicated client floor and a separate executive suite accommodate management facilities, enabling PTTEP to optimize workplace amenities. Two landscaped rooftop terraces provide additional space for employee recreation and corporate events. Mature trees and vertical climbers create a lush environment that complements the building's glass-framed walls. The layout of each floor features varied shapes that respond to client needs and enhance the building's architecture, resulting in a dynamic, comfortable space with notable aesthetic appeal.
