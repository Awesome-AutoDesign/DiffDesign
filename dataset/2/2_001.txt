Nature serves as a tranquil refuge for busy urban dwellers fatigued by concrete environments, with forests and mountains offering a calming influence that alleviates physical tension and mental stress. This design approach integrates natural, eco-friendly materials with artistic elements and greenery, symbolizing the arrival of spring and the renewal of life. Central to this concept is the theme of "leaves," which inspires the use of leaf shapes and patterns to craft a dining experience that feels both organic and inviting. This thoughtful design embodies the essence of spring, fostering a space that promotes comfort, rejuvenation, and a deeper connection to the natural world.
